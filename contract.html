Terms of Service

1. Order Process

By placing an order with Pixels on Paper, you agree to the following terms:

  • All orders require approval of digital proofs before printing begins.
  • Once you approve a proof, you accept responsibility for any errors that may exist in the approved design.
  • We reserve the right to refuse printing any content that we deem inappropriate or illegal.
  • Payment is required in full before printing begins.

2. File Requirements

To ensure the best quality prints, please follow these guidelines:

  • Files should be submitted in PDF, JPEG, TIFF, or PNG format.
  • All images should be at least 300 DPI at the final print size.
  • Files should include a 1/8" bleed on all sides when applicable.
  • Text should be converted to outlines/paths or all fonts must be embedded.
  • Color mode should be CMYK for best color reproduction.

3. Copyright & Intellectual Property

By submitting content for printing, you confirm that:

  • You own or have permission to use all content, including images, logos, and text.
  • You have secured any necessary licenses for fonts, stock images, or other elements.
  • You will indemnify Pixels on Paper against any claims related to copyright infringement.

4. Shipping & Delivery

Our shipping policies include:

  • Standard production time is 3-5 business days after proof approval.
  • Shipping times are estimated and not guaranteed.
  • We ship via trusted carriers with tracking information provided.
  • International orders may be subject to customs fees, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

5. Returns & Refunds

Our return policy is as follows:

  • We stand behind the quality of our products. If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of receipt.
  • Refunds or reprints will be issued for products that are damaged or defective due to our error.
  • We cannot accept returns or issue refunds for correctly printed items that match approved proofs.
  • Custom printed items cannot be returned unless they are damaged or defective.
